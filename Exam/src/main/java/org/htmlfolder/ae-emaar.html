If you wish to invest in Emaar shares:
Please register yourself as an investor with the Dubai Financial Market (DFM). Kindly contact DFM directly or email at customerservice@dfm.ae
After registering, you need to use one of the registered brokers of the Dubai Financial Market for trading.

For a list of registered brokers please visit: http://www.dfm.ae/members/brokers

Please note that some brokers offer a full service which includes your registration with DFM.
